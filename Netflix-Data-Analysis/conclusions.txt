Netflix Data Analysis - Key Findings

1. Netflix contains significantly more Movies than TV Shows. The Movies category has nearly 4,000 titles, while TV Shows account for around 1,900 titles.
2. The United States contributes the largest amount of content on Netflix. Other major contributors include India, the United Kingdom, Japan, and several other countries.
3. International Movies are the most common genre on Netflix, indicating strong demand for content from different regions of the world.
4. Drama is the second most common genre, followed by Comedies and other categories.
5. Content production and availability on Netflix increased dramatically after 2000. The growth accelerated further between 2015 and 2020, which represents the platform's most significant expansion period.
6. TV-MA is the most common content rating on Netflix, followed by TV-14. Other ratings such as TV-PG and R appear less frequently.
